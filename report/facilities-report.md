# Facilities Editability Report

This report outlines the editable content and limitations for various facility sections to ensure consistent layout and prevent overcrowding.

## 1. Central Computing

### Editable Content
- **Stats**: Lab count, computers, servers, labels.
- **Staff**: Names, roles.
- **Labs**: Lab name, PC count, images, configurations, specs.
- **Values**: All text and numeric values.

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Stat label: max 15 chars
  - Staff name: max 30 chars
  - Role: max 40 chars
  - Lab name: max 30 chars
  - Spec line: max 120 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Stats: max 4–5 items
  - Staff: max 6–8 members
  - Labs: max 5–6 labs

**Reason**: Prevents overcrowded cards and maintains grid alignment.

---

## 2. Counselling Cell

### Editable Content
- **Counsellor Information**: Name, role, details.
- **Mentor Record Items**: Various record entries.
- **Textual Content**: Headings, descriptions, services text.

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Counsellor name: max 30 chars
  - Role: max 40 chars
  - Description: 100–150 chars
  - Section heading: max 50 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Counsellors: max 4–6
  - Record items: max 6–8

**Reason**: Maintains clarity and prevents overcrowding.

---

## 3. Differently Abled

### Editable Content
- **Facility Names**: List of available facilities.
- **Descriptions**: Detailed explanation of facilities.
- **Text content**: All general text fields.

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Facility name: max 40 chars
  - Description: 100–120 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Per row: max 6 items
  - Total rows: fixed (3)

**Reason**: Extra items distort grid symmetry and spacing.

---

## 4. Health Facilities

### Editable Content
- **Entries**: Health facility names and descriptions.
- **Management**: Addition of new facilities and updating existing ones.

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Facility name: max 40 chars
  - Description: 120–150 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Facilities: max 4–6

**Reason**: Ensures proper layout structure, scalability, and balanced visual presentation.

---

## 5. Ladies Common Room

### Editable Content
- **Facility Info**: General information updates.
- **Indoor Activities**: List of activities and descriptions.
- **Text Content**: Descriptions and textual blocks.

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Title: max 30 chars
  - Description: 100–120 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Items: max 4–6

**Reason**: Maintains visual balance and avoids overcrowding.

---

## 6. Library (VCET Library Page)

### Editable Content
- **Text & Lists**: Headings, descriptions, facilities, memberships.
- **Governance**: Rules & regulations, committee members (names, roles).
- **Contact & Nav**: Phone, email, address, navigation labels.
- **Media**: Gallery images.
- **Stats**: Library statistics (total books, journals, etc.).

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Headings: 30–80 chars
  - Paragraphs: 150–1000 chars
  - List items (incl. rules): 20–120 chars
  - Navigation labels: 10–25 chars
  - Address: 100–250 chars
  - Library count values: 1–6 digits
  - Committee member name: 20–50 chars
  - Designation/role: 20–60 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Facilities: 5–15 items
  - Rules & regulations: 10–25 items
  - Memberships: 3–10 items
  - Navigation tabs: 5–10 items
  - Images: 5–20 items
  - Library statistics: 3–8 items
  - Committee members: 5–15 members

---

## 7. Sports & Gymkhana

### Editable Content
- **Sports & Competitions**: Names, icons, competition lists.
- **Records**: Student achievements, sports results, performance records.
- **Text Content**: All general text areas.

### Detailed Limitations
- **Character Limits**:
  - Sport name: max 25 chars
  - Achievement text: 100–120 chars
  - Result entry: max 80 chars
- **Item Limits**:
  - Achievements: max 6–10
  - Results per category: max 4–5 years
  - Tabs: max 6–7
  - Rules: max 8–10
  - Gallery images: max 6–8

**Reason**: Prevents clutter and ensures clear readability of achievements.
